Design Rationale

  • Compact pill: Small inline element that doesn't dominate the post. Uses muted background to blend in.
  • Shortened handle: Shows just @username without domain to save space. Full handle on hover/click.
  • Icon + text: Small repost icon (12px) followed by "@user reposted" in monospace.
  • Position: Above the author header, clearly indicating "this is a repost of the below content".
  • Trade-off: Very minimal, might be too subtle for some users. Good for power users who want clean timelines.
