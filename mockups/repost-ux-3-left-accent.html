Design Rationale

  • Left accent bar: 4px colored border instantly identifies reposts when scanning. Consistent with notification hierarchy pattern.
  • Compact reposter row: Small avatar (20px), icon, name, and time. Separated by dashed border from main content.
  • Two timestamps: Shows both when it was reposted (2h ago) and original post time (6h). Full context.
  • Clear separation: Reposter info is visually distinct from the original post author.
  • Trade-off: Takes a bit more vertical space than the pill, but provides clearer information hierarchy.
