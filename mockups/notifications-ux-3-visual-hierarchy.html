Visual Hierarchy

Concept: Use visual weight (borders, shadows, spacing, color accents) to signal importance. High-importance items have elevated cards with strong borders and shadows. Low-importance items are minimal inline entries with muted styling. A colored left-border accent provides quick visual scanning.

Design Rationale

  • High importance (Mentions/Replies): 2px border, 4px shadow, 4px left accent, badge label. Demands attention through visual weight.
  • Medium importance (Follows/Reposts): 1px border, no shadow, 3px left accent. Present but not demanding.
  • Low importance (Likes/Reactions): No border (dashed separator), 2px left accent, muted colors. Contained in a grouped area.
  • Left accent bar: Enables rapid scanning - users can quickly identify notification types without reading.
  • Shadow on hover: High-importance items have interactive feedback, reinforcing their importance.
  • Trade-off: Simple CSS-only implementation. No backend changes needed. Works with current data model.
